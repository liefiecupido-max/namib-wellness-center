Surgery Aftercare Support

Mild hyperbaric sessions (1.3-1.4 ATA) for selected post-operative recovery contexts, coordinated as adjunct support.

Adjunct wellness/recovery support. Results vary by individual. Not a substitute for medical care.

Where it may fit

For routine uncomplicated recovery, progress usually follows your surgeon's plan. In slower or more complex recoveries, mHBOT can be discussed as an additional supportive option.

Sessions should be coordinated with your surgeon or treating doctor when healing concerns are present.

Typical discussion points

  • Slow-healing surgical wounds
  • Tissue viability concerns after surgery
  • Complex healing environments

How Namib Wellness Center supports you

  • Pre-screen and contraindication check
  • Session planning around your post-op timeline
  • Tolerance and response tracking across the block

Important

Do not stop medications, dressings, wound care, or doctor follow-up unless your doctor instructs it.

Practical next step

If recovery is slower than expected, book a screening discussion and bring your operation summary or referral notes where available.

The goal is supportive recovery, realistic expectations, and good coordination with your existing care team.
