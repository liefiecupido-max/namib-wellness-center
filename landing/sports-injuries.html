Sports Injury Recovery Support

mHBOT as an adjunct tool for active people and athletes during demanding training and rehab blocks.

Adjunct wellness/recovery support. Results vary by individual. Not a substitute for medical care.

Evidence overview

For routine sports injury recovery, evidence is promising but variable. Individual response can differ meaningfully.

Best used together with physiotherapy, load management, sleep strategy, nutrition, and return-to-play planning.

Where clients explore support

  • Soft tissue recovery periods
  • Post-competition fatigue and soreness
  • High-load training cycles

Who this may suit

  • Athletes with recurring overload or delayed recovery
  • People in rehab for strains/sprains
  • Endurance or contact athletes with dense schedules

Expectations

No guaranteed timeline can be promised. We use structured blocks and practical progress reviews.

Practical next step

Bring your rehab/training schedule to screening so sessions can align with your broader performance plan.

Aim: better recovery readiness and consistency, not overpromised quick fixes.
